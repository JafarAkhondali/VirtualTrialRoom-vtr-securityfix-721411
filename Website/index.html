Introduction

What is the Virtual Trial Room?

VTR is a tool to display how clothing actually looks on you.

It's responsive, utilizing a Kinect to generate an precise 3-D model. This would be a perfect solution for those who want to see clothing before they actually buy it.

The Details
The Details
The Details

What makes it different from what is already out there?

It's accurate.

We take precise measurements using the Kinect. No more inputting values manually and estimating sizes. We know your perfect fit.

It's Easy

Just twirl around the Kinect once, pick your clothing, and you have an full 360 model of yourself.

Infinitely Customizable

We have a database of clothing that you can mix and match to fit your style.

Free

VTR was a project taken by UIUC students enrolled in CS 196. This projcet is a a labor of love, and we want to share our experience with you.
